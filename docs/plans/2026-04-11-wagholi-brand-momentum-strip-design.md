# Wagholi Brand Momentum Strip Design

**Goal:** Add a subtle premium announcement line inside the hero glass card, directly below the trust box and above the CTA, without changing the existing layout structure.

**Design direction:** Keep the current luxury-commercial tone intact. The new element should feel like a continuation of the trust box rather than a new section. It should read as a quiet forward-signal: more brands are on the way, momentum is building, and the project feels alive.

## Placement

- Inside the existing glass card
- Immediately below the trust box
- Immediately above the primary CTA
- No reordering of current hero content

## Copy

- `Many More Big Brands Coming`

## Visual treatment

- Compact centered strip
- Warm gold/ivory foreground to match the existing accent palette
- Low-contrast border and soft internal glow
- Fine divider accents on either side to make it feel intentional and balanced

## Motion

- One-time fade and lift on initial render
- Very subtle moving sheen across the strip
- No bounce, marquee, or looping positional motion that would pull attention away from the CTA

## Constraints

- Do not disturb the trust box layout
- Do not change card width, order, or CTA treatment
- Must work cleanly on mobile and desktop
- Must preserve the current premium cinematic feel
